Why Now?

The climate crisis is not coming — it's here. Logistics accounts for nearly 10% of global emissions. Acaya's mission is to revolutionize how perishables move — silently, intelligently, and sustainably. Our smart thermal packaging doesn't just save energy; it saves futures.

Featured Innovations

Acaya Hot Box

Maintains food above 68°C using PCM + active coils. Built for urban deliveries, military, and beyond.

Acaya Cold Chain Tej

Keeps contents at 2–8°C for 72+ hours without a compressor. Ideal for pharma, florals, and dairy.

Deep Tech Meets Clean Tech

Acaya’s IoT-enabled devices log temperature, GPS, and battery health in real time. Paired with AI for predictive rerouting and PCM optimization, we deliver not just goods, but confidence. All while eliminating diesel, foam, and food waste.

About Us

Founded by innovators from food, pharma, and engineering backgrounds, Acaya was born from a shared dream: to use climate tech to solve everyday logistical challenges — from a mother sending food to her child, to a hospital shipping insulin across deserts.
